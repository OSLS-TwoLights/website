The Two Lights Financial Planning Process

  1. 1

    Discovery

    We start with what matters most to you. Your goals, your priorities, your story.

  2. 2

    Data Gathering

    We collect and organize key financial information to understand your full picture.

  3. 3

    Fine-Tuning

    We refine assumptions and stress-test scenarios to align the plan with your goals.

  4. 4

    We review existing accounts, policies, and holdings for alignment and gaps.

  5. 5

    We walk you through our recommendations and collaborate on the right path forward.

  6. 6

    We monitor, review, and adapt the plan as your life and the world evolve.
